# 13 — Out of Scope (MVP 0.1)

The following are explicitly out of scope for MVP 0.1:

- Unity integration;
- NWN2 runtime integration;
- multiple cities;
- caravans;
- armies;
- clans;
- states/governments;
- wars;
- influence-zone strategy layer;
- detailed district/building simulation.

Any expansion requires a separate decision and planning step.
